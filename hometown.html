About TaiZhou

Taizhou City has a history of over 2100 years of urban construction. It was named Haiyang in the Qin Dynasty and Hailing in the Han Dynasty. The state was established during the Southern Tang Dynasty and Wenchang during the Northern Song Dynasty. During the Southern Tang Dynasty (937 AD), it was under the jurisdiction of the state and was known as "Taizhou". It integrated the charm of Wu, Chu, and Yue, and gathered the wind of the Yangtze River, Huai River, and Hai River. In August 1996, Taizhou City was established at the prefecture level. Taizhou is home to a wealth of talented people, with Shi Nai'an, Zheng Banqiao, and Mei Lanfang being outstanding representatives. There are many places of interest and historic sites, such as Guangxiao Temple, Confucian Worship Temple, Chenghuang Temple, Anding Academy, Rishiyuan, Wanghai Tower, Mei Lanfang Memorial Hall, and the People's Navy Birthplace Memorial Hall. There are ecological and natural scenic spots such as Qinhu Wetland, Qiandao cauliflower, Water Forest, Tiande Lake Park, and Ancient Ginkgo Forest.

Taizhou City is a water and land hub that connects the south and the north, serving as the gateway to Suzhong. It has been known since ancient times as a "water and land hub, with a throat based on the county."

Taizhou Tourist Attractions

Qinhu National Wetland Park

The Qinhu National Wetland Park is located in the northeast suburbs of Taizhou City, with a large lake surface and crisscrossing rivers, and water grass and reeds everywhere

Li Zhong Water Forest

The park was originally a wasteland. In the early 1980s, local officials and people developed the wasteland into a series of stacked grids for the rational development and utilization of wasteland resources

Qianduo Scenic Area

Legend has it that Qianduo was a gossip formation set up by the Jin army during the Battle of Taizhou Governor Yue Fei. Farmland is susceptible to waterlogging disasters...

Qintong Ancient Town

The ancient town is surrounded by water on all sides and has a pleasant environment. Every year during the Qingming Festival, the spectacular "Qintong Boat Festival" is held here...
